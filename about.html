About Nexiono

Next-Generation Cyber Security Built on Trust, Expertise, and Innovation

Our Story

Nexiono was founded in 2023 by a team of seasoned cyber security professionals who saw a growing gap in the market: small and medium-sized businesses were increasingly targeted by sophisticated cyber attacks, yet lacked access to affordable, enterprise-grade protection.

We believed that every organization — regardless of size — deserves the same level of security that Fortune 500 companies enjoy. Too many providers offered either basic antivirus solutions or overly complex (and expensive) services that overwhelmed smaller teams.

That's why we built Nexiono from the ground up as a fully managed 24/7 cyber security service, combining cutting-edge technology with human expertise. Our Security Operations Centre (SOC) monitors client environments around the clock, proactively hunting threats and responding within minutes — not hours or days.

Today, we protect over 250 organizations across multiple industries, from finance and healthcare to retail and professional services. Our mission remains unchanged: to make world-class cyber security simple, effective, and accessible.
