Our Core Cyber Security Services

Managed Detection & Response (MDR)

Our flagship MDR service combines advanced endpoint detection and response (EDR/XDR) technology with human-led threat hunting. We monitor your networks, endpoints, cloud environments, and logs 24/7, identifying and stopping threats in real time. From malware and ransomware to insider threats and zero-day exploits, our analysts respond within minutes to contain and remediate incidents.

Penetration Testing & Vulnerability Management

We simulate real-world attacks through ethical hacking to uncover weaknesses in your systems, applications, and infrastructure. Our certified pentesters deliver detailed reports with prioritized remediation steps. Continuous vulnerability scanning and patch management ensure your defenses stay ahead of emerging threats.

Security Awareness Training & Phishing Simulation

Human error remains the leading cause of breaches. Our comprehensive training programs educate employees on best practices, while regular phishing simulations test and improve their vigilance. We help build a strong human firewall across your entire organization with engaging, interactive content and measurable results.
